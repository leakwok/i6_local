Why I Like Astrophysics

When I was younger, I had a deep fascination with space and telescopes. I loved flipping through the pages of my dad's old astronomy textbook and analyzing the star charts in the back of the book all day. One specific memory I have is when I read about Mae Jemison, the first black woman to travel to space. I was in second grade, writing a book report about her, and I knew that was exactly what I wanted to do when I was older. Her story inspired me with her grit and ambition.
